Directory of Local Businesses

Foyles Bookshop at Charing Cross Road

Explore four miles (6.4km) of shelves holding more than 200,000 different titles at Foyles Bookshop in central London. This literary landmark, founded by brothers William and Gilbert Foyle, has been part of Charing Cross Road since 1906. Find books, stationery, magazines and gifts that inspire, educate and delight. Alongside books, there’s also plenty to discover in the music department including sheet music, classical music CDs and World DVDs.

Contact: 0987654321 - Location: 107 Charing Cross Road, London

Daunt Books Marylebone

Step into a world of books at Daunt Books on Marylebone High Street. Though its three floors are predominantly dedicated to travel guides, literature and maps from across the globe, the much-loved bookseller is more than a travel specialist. It also has an excellent selection of fiction, non-fiction and children’s books.

Lutyens & Rubinstein

Explore the shelves at independent bookshop Lutyens & Rubinstein, based in west London. The shop has plenty of fiction and non-fiction treasures to capture your imagination, alongside special sections for children’s books, poetry and art books. Stock up on stationery, cards and small gifts and take your precious purchases home in a shop-branded canvas tote bag.

Contact: 1234567890 - Location: 21 Kensington Park Road, Notting Hill, London
